Messages from 061

She can’t run

When the only person left
to root for her
is her.

She just can’t
be on those
blurry street corners anymore.

She can say,
“What’s done
is done.”

She can pull
the trigger
of that empty gun.

She can hide
from the past
but she can’t run.
