I want to be somebody to you

I wonder if you still feel me
in moments that flash by.
Not always, not often—just some of the time.

I wonder if you still see me dance in your cars,
or hear me talk louder at your bars,
or taste me on your pricey cigars.

Weeks, or months, or years flash by,
but I still feel you—
at least some of the time.

I guess,
even if it’s only momentary,
I’d hate to feel that fucking temporary.
