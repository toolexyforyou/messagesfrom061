Russian roulette

Go ahead, babe, do it.
Don’t put up a fight.
Be another “you deserve better”
by the end of the night.

Tell me pretty white lies
about how you only have eyes for me,
about how you never, ever
tasted nothing so sweet.

I know it’s Russian roulette
at the end of the night.
So go ahead, babe, disappoint me—
you do it just right.
